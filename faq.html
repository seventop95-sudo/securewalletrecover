We recover most major cryptocurrency wallets including: Software wallets (MetaMask, Blockchain.com, Electrum, Exodus, Bitcoin Core, MultiBit, MIST/Geth, Armory), Hardware wallets (Trezor, Ledger, KeepKey, ColdCard), and Mobile wallets (Trust Wallet, Coinbase Wallet, BRD, Schildbach). We support 50+ cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and all ERC-20 tokens.

We charge a 20% success fee only when we successfully recover your wallet. There are absolutely no upfront costs. The initial assessment is free, and if we cannot recover your wallet, you pay nothing. This ensures our interests are aligned with yours.

Recovery time varies based on complexity. Simple cases (good password hints): 1-7 days. Medium complexity: 1-4 weeks. Complex cases (minimal hints): Several weeks to months. We provide an estimated timeline after our free assessment.
